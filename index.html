Welcome to Mickey Dee's
Our family friendly restaurant was created by two founders: Mickey Donaldson and Dee's McKay.
The two were both starving on the streets of Ohio, and they found eachother wandering the streets. Combined, they had enough money to buy an abandoned building.
After 20 years of hard struggles, they finished the building.
They grew all the food the restaurants needed
It gave their food a unique tasting flavor, and their restaurant became one of the most famous in the world!
